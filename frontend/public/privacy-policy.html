1. Introduction

Tekisho Infotech Private Limited ("Tekisho", "we", "us", or "our") operates LeadQ, a cloud-based lead management and sales pipeline platform (the "Service" or "Platform").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect personal information when individuals or organizations access or use the Service.

LeadQ may be used by:

  • Businesses and organizations, and
  • Individual professionals, including freelancers, consultants, sole proprietors, and independent users.

By accessing or using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, you must discontinue use of the Service.

2. Scope and Roles

2.1 Applicability

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • All LeadQ websites and applications that link to this Policy
  • All users who create an account or otherwise interact with the Service

2.2 Data Roles

For clarity and compliance:

Customer / User

The individual or organization that uses LeadQ and determines how lead or contact data is collected and used.

Tekisho's Role

  • Data Fiduciary (Controller): For account information, billing data, platform administration, security, and marketing communications.
  • Data Processor: For lead, contact, and customer data uploaded by users into the Platform ("Customer Data").

This structure aligns with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India) and global data protection principles.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

We may collect the following categories of information:

Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name (if applicable)
  • Role or designation

Customer Data (Uploaded by You)

  • Lead names and contact details
  • Business contact information
  • Interaction notes, communications, and pipeline data

Payment and Billing Information

  • Billing name and address
  • Transaction identifiers
  • Payment method type

Tekisho does not store full credit card numbers, UPI credentials, CVV, or bank account details. Payments are processed securely by authorized payment processors.

Communications

  • Support requests
  • Emails or messages exchanged with our team

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access or use LeadQ, we automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device and operating system information
  • Log data (timestamps, error logs, access records)
  • Usage data such as features accessed and session duration

This information is used strictly for security, analytics, and platform improvement.

3.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive limited information from:

  • Payment processors
  • Authentication providers (if enabled)
  • Publicly available sources
  • Marketing or referral partners

Such data is used only for legitimate business purposes and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

4. How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the Service
  • Account creation, authentication, and administration
  • Processing subscriptions, payments, and invoices
  • Customer support and service communications
  • Platform security, fraud prevention, and abuse detection
  • Product improvement, analytics, and performance monitoring
  • Legal compliance and enforcement of our Terms
  • Sending essential service notices and, where permitted, marketing communications

Tekisho does not sell personal data and does not use customer data for third-party advertising.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Performance of a contract
  • User consent (where applicable)
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate business interests that do not override user rights

Where consent is required, you may withdraw it at any time.

6. Payment Processing

Payments are processed through Razorpay or other authorized payment processors compliant with PCI-DSS standards.

Tekisho retains only limited transaction metadata necessary for:

  • Accounting
  • Customer support
  • Dispute resolution

For details on Razorpay's practices, please refer to their privacy policy.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical, administrative, and organizational safeguards, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Controlled access based on role and necessity
  • Regular security assessments
  • Monitoring and logging of system activity
  • Employee confidentiality and security training

While we follow industry best practices, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.
